Swann Tubach

I am a postdoc at Jussieu, in the group of Javier Fresan, who funds me through his EMOTIVE grant. Before that, I was a PhD student under the supervision of Sophie Morel at the UMPA (ÉNS de Lyon).

My main interests are motivic, and I work with motivic sheaves of all sorts (Perverse Nori motives, étale motivic sheaves, mixed Hodge modules). I also like when ideas and methods of algebraic topology are used in algebraic geometry.
